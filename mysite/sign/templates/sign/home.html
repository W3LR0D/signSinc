Welcome to SignSync.

Empowering the World Beyond Words



We leverage our website as a powerful tool to assist individuals seeking to bridge the communication gap with those who are hearing impaired. According to data from the National Deaf Center, approximately 3.6% of the United States population, roughly 11 million people, are affected by significant hearing loss. That's equivalent to 36 out of every 1,000 individuals facing challenges in auditory communication. Our initiative, aptly named signSync, serves as a vital conduit facilitating effective communication between those proficient in American Sign Language (ASL) and those less familiar with it. SignSync offers simplified ASL constructs, designed to streamline basic communication for both fluent ASL users and novices alike.
